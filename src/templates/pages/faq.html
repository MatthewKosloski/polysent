What is Polysent?

Polysent is an online poll maker that allows you to instantly obtain anonymously-submitted results with ease.

How many polls can I submit?

You can submit as many polls as you desire; however, all submissions are subject to review.

Can I submit a similar poll that already exists?

Yes, feel free to submit a poll that has already been added to Polysent.

Can I create a private poll?

Yes, you don't have to create a public poll that will be added and archieved on the site for everyone to see. If you desire to create a private poll, just select "Private" on the form when submitting.

If I have a suggestion or found a bug, how do I contact you?

If you discover problems during your experience, feel free to create an issue on the Github repository, send me an email, or tweet me.
